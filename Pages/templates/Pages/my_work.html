WebSNES: The NES and SNES Web Emulator

  • Uses JavaScript for the emulation logic, which simulates the CPU, the memory, and the sound systems of both the NES and SNES consoles.
  • Makes use of the HTML5 Canvas to render games in the browser, while also preserving the original look and feel of the games.
  • Implements a secure system to load the games' ROMs through safe user uploads.
